About the Artist

Poet, wanderer, collector of moments. I believe every silence holds a story waiting to be heard, and every shadow carries light within it. My work explores the liminal spaces between memory and dream, between what we say and what we mean. When I'm not writing, you'll find me chasing golden hour or getting lost in bookshops.
